About US


We at Om Engineering manufacture low pressure hydraulic Non return valve(check valve). This valve is used in hydraulic Oil Systems. This valve is also designed for applications in major industries like hydraulics automation, power plants, Steel Plants, Mining Industry, Cement Plants, Petro chemical Industries, Earth moving Equipments, Ship Building, Furnace Manufacturing Industry and lot more. We are into supplying bulk orders to OE customers and also smaller quantities to individual customers/consumers. We believe in research for improving our products based on the customer feedback.
