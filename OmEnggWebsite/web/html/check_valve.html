Description
Om Engineering Low Pressure Non Return Valve (NRV) also known as Check Valve is designed one directional flow regulation. Om Engineering team has specialized themselves in Low Pressure application. This compact design Low Pressure Non Return Valve (NRV / Check Valve) are manufactured for Low Pressure up to 350bar (15000PSI) & have huge application in Low Pressure Hydraulic Systems.
Features of Om Engineering Low Pressure Non Return Valves (NRV) / Check Valve:
  • Working pressure up to 350 bar.
  • Hexagonal housing made of Toughend Body and Hardened Poppets.
  • Finish is Zinc Plating.
  • Are also manufactured in stainless steel
  • Prevents Flow in reverse Direction
Pressure Rating : up to 350 bar.
Size Range : 1/4” to 1 1/2”
Material of Construction : carbon steel / Stainless Steel
End Connection : Female BSP

Flow Media

 : Hydraulic Oil.
Application : Hydraulic Systems used in Material Handling Equipments,  Earth Moving Machineries, Power generating Plants, Steel Manufacturing Plants, Mining Industries, Forklifts, Trucks / Trailers, Chemical Processing Machines etc.
Choice of Cracking : S1 - 0.5 Bar
S2 - 1.5 Bar
S3 - 3.o Bar
S4 - 5.0 Bar
